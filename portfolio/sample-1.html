Genre/Audience

Originally, my group decided to mix the 2017 film, It, with the classic musical, Singing in the Rain. I loved this idea, but I decided to mix the horror element of It with the indie genre, particularly a Wes Anderson film. The It poster is quite dark, and Wes Anderson is known for pastel colors, so the juxtaposition of genres stands out. Since Wes Anderson is not as widely known to the mainstream—that sounds pretentious—than a horror film like It, so maybe making a short trailer to show more of the quirky cinematic touches of a Wes Anderson film, especially with music and symmetry.
