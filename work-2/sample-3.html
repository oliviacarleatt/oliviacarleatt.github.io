Original Soundscape

My group chose to do a soundscape based on what it would be like to be inside of a beer bottle. I chose a party atmosphere fro the voices in the background. Over that I mized sounds of bottle clinking and bottle caps, and gulping. I used an ambient under water sound to represent someone holding their breath. My revision is much cleaner and more precise. It follows a narravite more smoothly. The original was quite messy and confusing so I wanted to improve it for the second work in progress assignment.
